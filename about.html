About Soccer Mom Club

2020

Given the CoVID-19 situation, we have reduced the number of meet up sessions. As phase 3 is nearing, the committee has decided to regulate the meet up sessions but with only a maximum of 8 people per session. Session numbers would remain the same to minimize outside contact to keep you and your kids safe.

2019

There were a lot of soccer sessions held. Given the numerous number of moms that signed up for the club, the committee decided to have 2 weekly sessions. For more information on weekly session please click on 'Weekly Sessions' below.

2018

This year marks the first anniversary of Soccer Mom Club!As of 2018 we have 500 moms who have joined the club! To manage timings, booking slots and other admin matters, the committee was set up in 2018.

2017

This marks the year of the start of our Soccer Mom Club. We only had 1 session per week. Every session would only last for 1 hour with minimal activites. Moms who were keen to come had to travel all the way till Pasir Ris to take part.
